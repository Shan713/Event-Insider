About Event Insider

Event Insider is a pioneering startup based in Ettimadai, founded by the innovative minds of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore. Our mission is to revolutionize the way you experience events. Whether it's cultural fests, academic conferences, or vibrant exhibitions, Event Insider ensures you stay informed and engaged.

Our team consists of passionate students from diverse disciplines, united by a shared vision to create a seamless and engaging platform for event enthusiasts. We leverage cutting-edge technology and creative strategies to provide comprehensive event coverage, updates, and personalized recommendations.

At Event Insider, we believe in the power of community and connection. Our platform is designed to foster a sense of belonging and excitement, making every event accessible and memorable. Join us on our journey to bring the world of events closer to you!
